EBER HEALTH

THE PROCESS

The Goal

To allow users to order health and wellness services to the convenience of their home, or place of their choosing. Also, to allow practitioners to be able to find clients on the mobile application, and be able to do billing directly through application.

It all started with some research and brainstorming.

Once we started to understand the needs of the users, and gathered business requirements and guidelines from the stakeholders and CEO, It was time to start creating some low fidelity wireframes.

After completing low fidelity wireframes, we created a prototype which we would use in our user research. We conducted over 10 rounds of user research in which we conducted face to face interviews, and A/B testing.

After many iterations, and perfecting our mid-fidelity mockups, we created a site map in order to make sure all user flows were accounted for, and that no screens were missing.

After many more hours of user research, presentations, and many hours of wireframe iterations and design sessions, it was time to create high fidelity mockups.

Weekly findings and retrospectives were presented formally to CEO, stakeholders and engineering team.
